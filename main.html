About Us

PGARD is a data analytics platform designed to provide valuable insights from India's Census data. Our goal is to empower users with the ability to explore and understand various demographic trends, economic factors, and social issues affecting the country.

Features

  • Comprehensive data visualizations for easy understanding.
  • Search functionality to quickly find specific data points.
  • Filters to narrow down results by year, type of data (hospital, school, population), and more.
  • Feedback system to gather user insights and improve the platform.

Our Team

We are a group of passionate developers and data enthusiasts committed to making data accessible to everyone. Our team works tirelessly to ensure the platform is user-friendly and informative.
